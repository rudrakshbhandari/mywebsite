Projects

ShareAllBooks

Website: www.shareallbooks.com

ShareAllBooks is a mobile app I have developed using React Native and launched on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. It provides a platform for users to donate, sell, and buy books within the UAE. Its mission is to increase access to books (especially educational books) by making them more affordable, reduce the environmental impact of printing books, and build a vibrant community of readers.

Front-end: React Native. Back-end services: Firebase, EmailJS

ShareAllBooks has been ranked up #41 in the Apple App Store's Top Charts under the "Book" category. Hundreds of books have been donated through ShareAllBooks. Download the ShareAllBooks App and join us!
