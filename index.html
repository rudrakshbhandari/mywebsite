Projects

ShareAllBooks

Website: www.shareallbooks.com

ShareAllBooks is a mobile app I have developed using React Native and launched on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. It provides a platform for users to donate, sell, and buy books in the UAE. Its mission is to increase access to books (especially educational books) by making them more affordable, reduce the environmetal impact of printing books, and build a vibrant community of readers.
